Use this after the automated proof to judge whether the room interaction changes the next play beat, not just the final board.

- After using Rest Shrine, do I know whether HP recovery actually happened or whether the party was already healthy?

## Greed Cache

- After opening Greed Cache, do I know I gained real `mana_shard` and accepted pressure?
- Does `Goblin Pair Hall` acknowledge `Cache Pressure` before or during battle?
- Does the encounter popover confirm `Cache Check` with reward secured and strain warning visible?

## Result And World

- Does the final result still match the actual returned shard count?
- Does the CityHub/world board preserve the room consequence without duplicating too much text?
